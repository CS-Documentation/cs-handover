How to complete this section:

For each risk domain, work through the three steps in order. The goal is not simply to match two risk levels together, but to assess the real impact this placement would have on the household and on the new young person.

A high risk on both sides does not automatically mean the placement is unsuitable – it depends on what is already in place and whether the placement introduces something new or adds to something already being managed.
